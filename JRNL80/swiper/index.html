Belmont Hosts Spring Craft Beer Festival

by Joseph Pantaleo and Victoria Conway

Local Long Islanders particpate in a beer sampling, featuring over 100 types of beer.

In their 12th annual spring festival, event-goers taste-tested beer from both new and award-winning breweries from Long Island, as well as craft breweries from across the country.

Those who paid for VIP Admission were given a 5oz tasting glass and an extra hour to enjoy the festival before the General Admission ticketholders entered at 1:30pm.

Oskar Blue Brewery -- a Colorado-based company known for selling a wide-range of beers and IPAs -- nearly sold-out their inventory by the end of the day.

The beer festival also took place during the opening weekend at Belmont Raceway's 150th season.

Thousands of other showed up to Belmont for the world-class horse-racing and gambling experience.

Belmont Raceway is looking forward to another great season, hosting the highly-anticiatped third-leg of the triple crown on June 9th.
